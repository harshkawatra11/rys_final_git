Who We Are

The Spirit of RYS

Our Vision

"संवाद ही हल है।"

— Dialogue is the only solution.

Rajdhani Yuva Sansad (RYS) was founded in 2017 by two young law students with a vision of nation building through imparting quality education by unconventional and interesting means.

The organisation began at the grassroot level, with its founding members serving as Executive Board and Jury members at conferences held across various cities including Delhi, Ghaziabad, Jodhpur, Sangrur, Moradabad, Ujjain, Rohtak, and Sonipat.

Following this foundational experience, the team began visiting schools across Delhi NCR, proposing and initiating Youth Parliaments and Model United Nations sessions for students of middle school and high school.

Through this platform, RYS aims to instill in its participants the values of critical thinking, respectful discourse, and a deep understanding of national issues, empowering them to be the torchbearers of change.

Our Legacy

From 2017 to Today

2017

Founding Year

It came into being as the vision of Adv. Isha Kapoor and Adv. Jai Saini was brought to life, rooted in the belief that "संवाद ही हल है।" Driven by an unwavering zeal to educate, empower, and engage the youth, it has grown into a platform where dialogue shapes perspective, voices find meaning, and young leaders are inspired to create impact through informed discourse.

2018

Fostering Early Dialogue

At St. Xavier's School, Civil Lines, Delhi, over 250 students came together for a vibrant exchange of ideas on pressing national and global issues. At Salwan Public School, Gurugram, an immersive mock parliamentary session was conducted, offering students hands-on experience of democratic governance.

2019

Sajag Yuva Manch 1.0

At Silverline Prestige School, Ghaziabad, around 250 students engaged in dynamic debates on diverse issues. A distinctive highlight involved deep explorations of India's partition, and the introduction of the 'Memer' portfolio in the International Press added an impactful, contemporary edge to traditional discourse.

2021

Sajag Yuva Manch 2.0

Sajag Yuva Manch 2.0 brought together ~250 students for carrying forward strong traditions of collaboration. At Bal Bharti Public School, Noida, an immersive mock parliament enabled students to navigate legislative procedures, greatly enhancing teamwork and public speaking skills.

2022

1st Lawyers' Parliament Festival

A monumental milestone bringing ~350 lawyers for a deeply enriching UCC discussion with Hon'ble Judges of the Delhi High Court. Followed by Sajag Yuva Manch 3.0 witnessing an impressive gathering of ~350 students to nurture diplomacy and critical thinking.

2023

Sajag Yuva Manch 4.0

Witnessed extraordinary participation from 500 students. Introduced a fictional superhero committee to uniquely blend imagination and intellect. Further extended vision with intensive parliamentary training at H.P. Children's Academy, Gorakhpur.

2024

A Defining Year of Scale

The historic Delhi Yuva Samvad at Vidhan Sabha brought 75 exceptional leaders. The 2nd National Lawyers' Parliament Festival at IIC saw ~500 legal professionals discussing New Criminal Laws. SYM 5.0 hosted 700+ delegates. Global Panchayat 1.0 and Bal Sansad 1.0 nurtured civic awareness.

2025

Massive Expansion

The 1st Haryana Yuva Samvad at Vidhan Sabha, Chandigarh. Gorakhpur Yuva Samvad emerged as a landmark youth confluence with 900+ delegates across 19 committees. SYM 6.0 engaged 600+ delegates with inspiring guests, alongside successfully executing Global Panchayat 2.0 and Bal Sansad 2.0.

2026

Continuing the Legacy

The 2nd Haryana Yuva Samvad held in Jan 2026 at Haryana Vidhan Sabha brought 70 young participants for rigorous legislative engagement in the premises of governance. The year is all set to witness SYM 6.0, Global Panchayat 3.0, Bal Sansad 3.0, and Yuva Sajhedari 3.0.
