About the Conference

Youth Voices at the House of the Nation

The Amrit Kaal National Youth Parliament was held on 23rd December 2023 at the Parliament of India — a rare and prestigious venue that symbolised the scale and significance of the event. Open to all youth between 15 and 24 years of age at absolutely no cost, the event welcomed over 1,000 participants.

The agenda centred on the theme of Amrit Kaal: Building a Developed Bharat by 2047. Young leaders voiced their visions, debated policy priorities, and submitted concrete recommendations on India's future development trajectory — directly engaging with some of the country's most prominent leaders.

What Made This Event Special

  • Held at the iconic Parliament of India — highest-prestige venue
  • 1000+ participants from across the country — open registration
