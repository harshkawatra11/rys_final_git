About the Conference

Bringing Parliament to School

As part of RYS's school outreach programme, a series of Mock Lok Sabha sessions were conducted at Bal Bharti Public School, Sector-21, Noida during 2021–2022. These in-school events allowed students from Classes 9 to 12 to step into the roles of Members of Parliament and experience legislative debate firsthand.

The school-based format enabled wider participation beyond the formal conference circuit, bringing the experience of parliamentary democracy into the classroom and nurturing a new generation of civic-minded young leaders.

Key Highlights

What Made This Event Special

  • School-based Mock Lok Sabha sessions for Classes 9–12
  • Brought parliamentary simulation directly into the school environment
  • Students assumed roles of MPs from diverse constituencies and parties
  • Debates centred on contemporary legislative and social policy issues
  • Part of RYS's in-school outreach to democratise access to parliamentary education
  • Built a feeder community of strong participants for future SYM conferences
