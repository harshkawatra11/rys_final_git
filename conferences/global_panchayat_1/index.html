About the Conference

Dialogue, Not Division: The Power of Diplomacy

The First Edition of Global Panchayat 1.0, organised by Rajdhani Yuva Sansad, was held from 24th to 25th October 2024 at JKG International School, Indirapuram, Ghaziabad. The event brought together a diverse group of students, educators, and professionals to engage in dynamic debates centred around pressing global and national issues.

Featuring 5 committees including the signature Marvel Commission on Superhuman Activities — a creative innovation blending pop culture with policy debate — the conference provided an immersive experience for 400+ participants.

5 Committees

Diverse Forums of Dialogue

UNGA
United Nations General Assembly
Global Cybersecurity with Emphasis on Child Safety

Representatives deliberated on alarming increases in cyber offences targeting children, calling for international cooperation and unified legal frameworks.

LS
Lok Sabha
Gender-Based Violence in India

Focused on urgent measures to improve women's safety, comprehensive policy reforms, and strengthened accountability mechanisms.

SAI
Sports Authority of India
Sports in Education & Mental Health

Addressed the mental health crisis among youth and the role of sports in improving well-being and student development.

MV
Marvel Commission
Bullying, Harassment & Diversity

Students assumed roles of superhumans debating ethical governance of extraordinary abilities, highlighting diversity and representation.
