About the Conference

Taking Parliamentary Education Beyond Delhi

In 2023, Rajdhani Yuva Sansad extended its mission of civic education beyond the national capital with a dedicated training programme at H.P. Children's Academy, Gorakhpur. These sessions introduced students from Classes 9 to 12 to the fundamentals of parliamentary procedure, MUN diplomacy, and public speaking.

This outreach initiative was part of RYS's broader vision to democratise access to parliamentary debate education and empower students in cities across India beyond the metro belt.

Key Highlights

What Made This Event Special

  • Training sessions for Classes 9–12 at H.P. Children's Academy, Gorakhpur
  • Focus on parliamentary debate, MUN diplomacy, and public speaking foundations
  • Part of RYS's outreach mission to extend civic education beyond Delhi
  • Interactive workshop format, personalised guidance from experienced MUNers
  • Students received certificates and were encouraged to participate in future SYM events
  • Exemplifying RYS's commitment to inclusive youth engagement across India
