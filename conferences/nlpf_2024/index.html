About the Conference

India's Premier Legal Youth Parliament

The Second National Lawyers' Parliament Festival took place on 24th November 2024 at the India International Centre, New Delhi. The agenda was focused on the discussion of the New Criminal Laws, 2023 — a landmark legislative development in India's criminal justice system.

Around 500 lawyers and law students participated, selected from over 1,000 applications. The suggestions and insights from the conference were compiled into a booklet that was subsequently recommended to relevant stakeholders — demonstrating direct real-world policy impact.

Key Highlights

What Made This Event Special

  • ~500 lawyers and law students from 1,000+ applications — highly competitive selection
  • Graced by Shri Arjun Ram Meghwal, Hon'ble Union Minister of Law and Justice
  • Attended by Hon'ble Judges from the High Court of Delhi
  • Agenda: India's New Criminal Laws, 2023 — deliberation and reform proposals
