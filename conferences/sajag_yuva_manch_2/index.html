About the Conference

Building on the Foundation

Sajag Yuva Manch 2.0 was held in 2021, expanding the conference to 250 students and reinforcing the multi-committee format established in the inaugural edition. Despite challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic period, the conference was successfully organised, demonstrating RYS's commitment to maintaining continuity of youth civic education.

This edition refined the committee structures and introduced more sophisticated policy agendas, preparing students for larger MUN and parliamentary debate opportunities at the national level.

Key Highlights

What Made This Event Special

  • 250 students from diverse schools in the NCR region
  • Built upon the success of the inaugural SYM 1.0 in 2019
  • Refined committee structures with more complex policy agendas
  • Organised during a challenging period following the onset of the COVID-19 pandemic
  • Demonstrated RYS's resilience and adaptability in youth outreach
  • Trained hundreds of students in parliamentary debate and MUN procedure
