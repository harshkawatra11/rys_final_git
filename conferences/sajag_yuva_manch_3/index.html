About the Conference

A Post-Pandemic Resurgence

Sajag Yuva Manch 3.0 marked a triumphant return to in-person conferencing in 2022 following the pandemic years. With approximately 350 students in attendance, the conference showcased the resilience and adaptability of the youth leadership community.

This edition built upon the foundations of SYM 1.0 and 2.0, reinforcing multi-committee formats with enhanced parliamentary procedure training and more challenging diplomatic agendas for student delegates.

Key Highlights

What Made This Event Special

  • ~350 students from schools across NCR participating
  • Marked the series' post-pandemic in-person return in 2022
  • Multiple committees covering parliamentary, diplomatic, and historic simulations
  • Strengthened parliamentary procedure training with experienced chairs
  • Expanded participation base from the previous edition
  • Continued the legacy of the SYM annual series with refreshed agendas
