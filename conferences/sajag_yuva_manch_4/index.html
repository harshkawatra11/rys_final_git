About the Conference

Innovation Meets Tradition

Sajag Yuva Manch 4.0 saw the RYS flagship series reach new heights with approximately 500 students gathering for two days of structured debate, diplomacy, and parliamentary simulation. Building on the success of previous editions, this conference introduced an exciting new committee: the Marvel Commission on Superheroes — which rapidly became one of the most popular formats in the SYM series.

The Marvel committee allowed students to engage with ethics, governance, and human rights through the lens of pop culture, making policy debates more accessible and engaging for young participants.

Key Highlights

What Made This Event Special

  • ~500 students from schools across NCR participating
  • Introduced the Marvel Commission on Superheroes — now a signature committee
  • Classic committees: Lok Sabha, UNGA, Historical Security Council, International Press
  • Marvel committee focused on ethical governance and human rights through pop culture
  • Students displayed exceptional debate, research, and public speaking skills
  • Continued expansion of the SYM series with increased school participation
