About the Conference

One of RYS's Earliest Conferences

The conference at St. Xavier's School, Civil Lines, New Delhi in 2018 stands as one of the earliest events in Rajdhani Yuva Sansad's history. Welcoming approximately 250 students from across Delhi schools, this event helped establish the organisational framework and conference culture that would define RYS for years to come.

Featuring three committees including a Historical simulation of the 1975 Emergency, the event offered students a rigorous yet accessible introduction to parliamentary and diplomatic debate formats.

Key Highlights

What Made This Event Special

  • One of RYS's earliest large-scale conferences — held in 2018
  • 250 students from Delhi schools participating
  • 3 committees: UNGA, Lok Sabha, and Historical Crisis (1975 Emergency)
  • Helped establish RYS's conference framework and organisational culture
  • Located at the historic St. Xavier's School, Civil Lines, New Delhi
  • Served as a foundation for the expanded SYM series launched in 2019

Conference Format

UNGA

International Affairs & Global Challenges

Delegates represented UN member nations, deliberating on pressing global issues through the lens of international diplomacy and negotiation.

LS

National Legislative Debate

Students debated contemporary national legislation in a simulated Lower House of Parliament setting.

HSM

A profound historical simulation revisiting the constitutional crisis of 1975, exploring democracy, civil liberties, and political power.
