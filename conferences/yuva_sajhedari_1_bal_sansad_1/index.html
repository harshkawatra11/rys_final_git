About the Conference

Nurturing Tomorrow's Leaders from an Early Age

In August 2024, Rajdhani Yuva Sansad launched two new inclusive youth outreach programmes: Yuva Sajhedari 1.0 and Bal Sansad 1.0. These events were designed to introduce younger students — from Class 3 to Class 7 — to the world of parliamentary democracy, diplomacy, and public speaking.

Yuva Sajhedari 1.0, held on August 9, welcomed ~100 participants from Classes 5–7, providing an introductory session on how the Parliament and UN function. Bal Sansad 1.0, held on August 7, engaged ~150 students from Classes 3–5 through an immersive, age-appropriate mock parliamentary experience including a lively International Press simulation.

What Made This Event Special

  • Yuva Sajhedari 1.0 (Aug 9): ~100 participants from Classes 5–7
  • Bal Sansad 1.0 (Aug 7): ~150 participants from Classes 3–5

  • Age-appropriate simulation formats for junior school students

  • Part of RYS's commitment to grassroots civic education from an early age
