About the Conference

A Vibrant Platform for Junior Leaders

Yuva Sajhedari 2.0 was a vibrant one-day conference designed for junior students, featuring 4 engaging committees that encouraged young minds to explore ideas of leadership, teamwork, and dialogue. The event provided an interactive platform for students to understand decision-making and express their views on various social and global issues in a structured yet fun environment.

Yuva Sajhedari 2.0 successfully inspired the next generation of changemakers, leaving every participant more aware, expressive, and eager to contribute positively to society.

Key Highlights

What Made This Event Special

  • 4 engaging committees tailored for junior student participants
  • Focus on nurturing confidence, communication, and collaboration among youth
  • Interactive discussions on social and global issues in a structured, fun format
  • Inspired the next generation of changemakers
  • Concluded with great enthusiasm and recognition of outstanding participants
